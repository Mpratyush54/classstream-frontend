Email Sending & Anti-Abuse Policy

Last Updated: 29 November 2025

This Email Sending Policy explains how Class Stream uses email for communication. We follow industry standards and Amazon SES requirements to ensure responsible email practices.

1. Purpose of Emails

Class Stream sends emails ONLY for essential, platform-related purposes such as:

  • Account verification and login alerts
  • Password reset messages
  • Transactional notifications (new lecture uploaded, class material updates)
  • Teacher live session alerts
  • Internal administrative or system messages

We do not send promotional, marketing, or newsletter-style emails.

2. Consent & Email Collection

Emails are collected only when a user voluntarily registers on the platform. We do not use purchased or third-party email lists.

All users provide explicit consent to receive platform-related notifications.

3. Bounce & Complaint Management

We use Amazon SES feedback loops to automatically:

  • Handle bounces
  • Handle spam complaints
  • Suppress invalid or problematic email addresses

Once an email address bounces or complains, it is automatically prevented from receiving future emails.

4. Anti-Spam Commitment

Class Stream is strictly anti-spam. We ensure:

  • No unsolicited email will ever be sent
  • No bulk marketing emails are sent
  • No third-party list sharing or selling
  • All content is user-requested and directly related to platform usage
