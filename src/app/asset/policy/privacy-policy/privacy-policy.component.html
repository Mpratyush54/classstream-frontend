Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 29 November 2025

Welcome to Class Stream. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services through pratyushes.dev.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when necessary, including:

  • Email address
  • Name and account details
  • Usage activity such as lecture views and interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for operational and educational purposes, such as:

  • Account verification and login security alerts
  • Transactional notifications (e.g., new lecture uploaded)
  • Teacher live session alerts
  • Internal communication related to platform activity

We do not send marketing or promotional emails.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is securely stored in our controlled backend systems. Access is limited to authorized personnel and is protected using industry-standard security practices.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third-party advertisers. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers used to deliver essential platform features (such as email delivery).

5. User Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your account data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Permanent deletion of your account

6. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as the account remains active. Upon request, account data can be permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Compliance

Class Stream complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP 2023) and follows GDPR-aligned principles such as data minimization, purpose limitation, and transparency.
